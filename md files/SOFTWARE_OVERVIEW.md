# Mikana Branch Guidebook - Software Overview

## Executive Summary

**Mikana Branch Guidebook** is a comprehensive business operations management platform designed for multi-location food service businesses. It digitizes and automates key operational processes across 12+ branch locations and a central production kitchen, transforming paper-based workflows into streamlined digital solutions.

---

## What the Software Does

### üè¢ Multi-Branch Operations Management

The platform serves as a **centralized hub** for managing all aspects of branch operations:

- **12+ Branch Locations**: Complete digital coverage of all Mikana branch locations across UAE (Dubai, Sharjah, RAK, Abu Dhabi, Ajman, Al Ain, Ruwais)
- **Central Kitchen Integration**: Dedicated production facility management with recipe management, production scheduling, and dispatch coordination
- **Role-Based Access**: Different dashboards and permissions for Admins, Operations Leads, Dispatchers, Central Kitchen staff, Branch Managers, and Branch Staff
